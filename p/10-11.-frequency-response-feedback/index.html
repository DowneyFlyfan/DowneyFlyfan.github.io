The Method of Short-Circuit Time Constants

  1. Let $V_{sig}= 0$

  2. Consider the capacitors one at a time. That is,while considering capacitor $C_i$ , replace all the other capacitors with short circuits.

  3. For each capacitor $C_i$, find the total resistance $R_i$ seen by $C_i$

  4. Calculate the 3-dB frequency $f_L$

$$ f_L = \sum_{i=1}^n\frac{1}{R_i C_i} $$
