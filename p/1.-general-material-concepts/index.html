Energy Band & Charge Carriers

Energy band Formation

  1. When atoms combine to form substances, the outermost shells, subshells and orbitals merge, providing a greater number of available energy levels for electrons to assume.

  2. When large numbers of atoms are close to each other, these available energy levels form a nearly continuous band wherein electrons may move.
